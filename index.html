This Cookie Policy explains how Maina – The Voice of Care ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you use our mobile application or visit related services provided under the name Maina.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you use a website or app. They help us provide, protect, and improve our services by giving us insight into how the services are being used.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the app to function properly. They enable core functionality such as reminders, appointments, and login access.
  • Analytics Cookies: These help us understand how users interact with our app. For example, Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics may be used to track usage patterns.
  • Advertising Cookies: We may use cookies from advertising partners like Google AdMob to display relevant ads to you.
  • Preference Cookies: These allow us to remember choices you’ve made, such as language preference or notification settings.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services we use, such as analytics and advertising providers. These providers may use cookies to collect information about your online activities across other apps or websites.

4. Your Cookie Choices

You have the option to control and limit how cookies are used on your device through your device settings or browser settings. You can clear or disable cookies anytime; however, this may impact the functionality of the app.

5. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
